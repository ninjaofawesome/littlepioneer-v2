What We Can Do

The Process

Even if you only sing, bring your lyrics and we'll work together to create the melody and music - just your voice and me on acoustic guitar or piano: once the foundation is down, we can build... or keep it sparse: to me it’s all about what’s best for the song. Everything I do is a collaboration, whether I’m playing the instrument or not, so I don’t do anything without my artists here giving their input/approval every step of the way. I'm an artist too, so I've had my own recording nightmares in the past with slow and unsatisfying experiences. I'm also glad to answer any questions about the recording process.

I do this full time, 7 days a week, so as long as you book a few weeks in advance you can get any slot you’d like, but feel free to get in touch to come chill and check out the place first: I don’t charge for the "hang session".

Collaboration

I have new school gear and software but old school production sensibilities so I’m interested in more than just setting up the mics and recording. I like to spend some time discussing what you hear in your head and/or your ideal sound and we will make that happen...but there’s no need for a fully realized "grand plan" either! By getting to know you and the music you love I can craft music that "fits" you, in any genre. It’s always completely collaborative, loose and fun.

I can produce, mix, master and flesh out your songs in my comfortable loft recording studio, located in Clinton Hill, only six subway stops into Brooklyn from NYC (and one block from Pratt Institute). All sessions are one on one (no assistants), which equals one unified goal: creating the coolest music we can, and only doing what’s best for each song.

Client Testimonials
